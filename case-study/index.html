About heyNoah.ai

heyNoah.ai is an AI-powered therapist app helping users talk through thoughts, manage anxiety, and receive mental health guidance without waiting for human therapists.

The brand has grown rapidly through social media, but after Meta’s Andromeda update, performance started dipping due to shifting creative signals and algorithmic preferences.

About Muze

Muze is an AI Performance Marketer that automates ad analysis, creative strategy, and budget optimization across Meta, Google, and TikTok Ads.

It acts like an autonomous media buyer — identifying what’s working, optimizing budgets in real time, and even generating new creatives using AI.

Muze helps DTC and app brands:

  • Cut ad wastage by up to 30–40%
  • Discover new winning ad angles using competitive + creative intelligence
  • Automate ad testing and creative refresh cycles without manual work

Summary & Problem Statement

  • Rising CACs: Post-Andromeda, ad performance dropped due to creative fatigue and lower CTRs.
  • Lack of Creative Volume: The internal team couldn’t produce enough new creatives fast enough to test angles and hooks.
  • Budget Waste: 25–30% of the ad spend was going to underperforming campaigns that were not getting conversions.

Agents Deployed

Creative Process Breakdown

1️⃣ Creative Intelligence (Learning from Past Data)

Problem: The team didn’t know which messaging angles or visuals truly resonated post-update.
Solution: Muze’s AI analyzed 100+ creative elements (tone, color, hook phrasing, intro timing) and found that:

  • Ads starting with “You’re not alone” drove 2.1x higher CTR.
  • Calm-toned, face-to-camera videos outperformed text-heavy creatives by 38%.

2️⃣ Competitor Insights (Finding New Angles)

Muze’s Competitive Analyzer identified top ad trends from other therapy and habit-building apps.
Example: Hooks like “Talk without judgment” and “Therapy that listens back” were driving high engagement — which Muze adapted into heyNoah’s creative direction.

3️⃣ Creative Generation (Scaling Content Volume)

Muze generated over 40 new ad concepts across formats:

  • Static ads with emotional affirmations
  • Short-form videos (UGC-style) introducing the AI therapist
  • Carousel creatives highlighting different “moods” or “use cases”

4️⃣ Ad Optimization & Spend Allocation

Once performance data started rolling in, Muze automatically:

  • Flagged ads with high CPC or low CTR as “fatigue risk”
  • Reallocated 20% of ad spend to top-performing campaigns
  • Auto-paused underperforming creatives without waiting for manual review
