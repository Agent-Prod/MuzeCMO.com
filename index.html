FAQ

What is Muze AI?

Muze AI is an all-in-one AI-powered performance marketing platform that runs and optimizes Meta Ads and Google Ads end-to-end. It replaces agencies, tools, and manual workflows with an autonomous AI ads team.

How is Muze AI different from other AI advertising tools?

Most AI tools only generate ad content. Muze AI manages the entire performance marketing workflow, including strategy, creatives, audience targeting, budget optimization, and continuous performance improvement.

Do I need performance marketing or technical knowledge to use Muze AI?

No. Muze AI is designed for founders and teams without marketing expertise. The platform handles everything automatically, without the need for agencies or in-house teams.

Which ad platforms does Muze AI support?

Muze AI supports Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram) and Google Ads, with full performance management across both platforms.

What kind of ads can Muze AI create?

Muze AI creates image ads, video ads, short-form videos, and B-roll creatives optimized for Meta Ads and Google Ads. The AI continuously tests and scales the best-performing creative formats automatically.

Can Muze AI manage and optimize existing ad accounts?

Yes. Muze AI audits existing Meta and Google Ads accounts, identifies what to pause, fix, and scale, and continuously optimizes campaigns for better results.

Does Muze AI replace an ad agency or in-house team?

Yes. Muze AI is built to replace performance marketing agencies and in-house ad teams by running ads end-to-end at a significantly lower cost.

Do I need a website to use Muze AI?

A website helps Muze AI learn your brand and conversion signals faster. In some cases, limited setup may still be possible without a website.

Is Muze AI better than using ChatGPT for ads?

ChatGPT helps with ideas. Muze AI runs real performance marketing, directly managing Meta and Google Ads with live data, optimization, and scaling.
