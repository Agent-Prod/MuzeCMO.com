FAQ

What happens when I connect my account?

Muze instantly audits your live campaigns — identifying wasted spend, fatiguing creatives, and underperforming audiences. You'll see exactly what's costing you money, before you spend anything on optimization.

Is the $49 plan really monthly?

Yes, $49/month with no lock-in. Cancel anytime. It's designed so you can experience Muze's creative quality and get a real audit before committing to full autonomy.

What are credits?

Credits are used for AI creative generation. On the $399 plan you get 400 credits/month — roughly 40–50 videos or 200 image ads. Credits reset monthly and don't roll over.

Does Muze work for agencies?

Yes. The $999+ AI Growth Operator plan includes white-label capabilities and a 30% revenue share model for agencies that resell Muze to their clients.

What ad platforms are supported?

Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram) and Google Ads (Search, Shopping, PMAX) are fully supported. Shopify integration is live on the App Store. TikTok Ads coming Q1–Q2 2026.
