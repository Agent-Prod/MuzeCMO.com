3.3 To Communicate with You

3.4 For Business Operations

  • Process payments and billing

3.5 No AI Model Training on Your Data

We do NOT use your data to train any AI or machine learning models. To be explicit:

  • Your ad creatives, campaign data, performance metrics, and business information are NEVER used to train, fine-tune, or improve any AI/LLM models

  • Your data is NEVER sold, licensed, or shared with third parties for their AI training purposes

  • Your data is NEVER used to benefit other customers or competitors

  • Your data is NEVER shared with AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) for model training

Muze is a software tool that uses AI services to process your requests in real-time. Your data is processed only to deliver the specific features you use (e.g., generating an ad, analyzing a campaign) and is not retained by AI providers for training purposes.

The only data we analyze internally is anonymized, aggregated platform usage data (such as which features are most popular) to improve the Muze software interfacenever your ad account data, creatives, or campaign information.

3.6 Your Data, Your Access

Your advertising data, creatives, and campaign information are accessible only to you. Muze employees do not access your ad account data except:

  • When you explicitly request technical support

  • To investigate reported security incidents

  • When required by law

We maintain strict access controls and audit logs for any such access. You maintain full ownership and control of all data you upload or connect to Muze.

  • Are bound by data processing agreements that prohibit unauthorized use

Your data passes through these services only momentarily to deliver the feature you requested.

6.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)

  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication

  • Infrastructure: Secure cloud hosting with SOC 2 compliant providers

  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and intrusion detection

  • Audits: Regular security assessments and penetration testing


6.2 Employee Access

  • Access to user data is limited to authorized personnel

  • Employees are trained on data protection and privacy

  • Access is logged and monitored


6.3 Incident Response

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information:

  • We will investigate and contain the breach

  • We will notify you within 72 hours if required by law

  • We will provide information about the breach and steps you can take

  • We will report to relevant authorities as required


6.4 Limitations

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
